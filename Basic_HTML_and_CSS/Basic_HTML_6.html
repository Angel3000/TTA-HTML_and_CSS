The Yearly Climate In Madrid - July 2019

Due to Madrid's high altitude of 650 metres, it experiences quite different temperatures in the summer and the winter.
The most pleasant warm weather falls between May and mid July when average temperatures are between 20 and 32 degrees Celsius (68°F - 90°F).

Towards the end of July, and throughout August, it can get hot with temperatures sometimes reaching 40 degrees Celsius (100°F) or even more.
